Sensors Explained

  • PIR(Passive Infrared Motion Detectors) - PIR sensors can efficiently detect changes in the ambient temperature as well as body heat
  • US(Ultrasonic Sensor) - Ultrasonic detectors use high-frequency sound waves to detect any kind of movement within a limited space
  • MD(Magnetic - Detectors) Switches are typically used for protecting doors and windows. The switch creates an electrical circuit with the magnet when closed because it contains a spring. If a window or door is opened the circuit breaks and the alarm is triggered.
  • HAP(House Alarm Panel) - This is the house's main panel this status depends on if all sensors are On or Off.
